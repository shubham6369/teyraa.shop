Faq's

Every timepiece undergoes a final 24-hour precision test before dispatch. We usually deliver within 5–8 working days. Once your watch is shipped, you'll receive white-glove tracking details via WhatsApp/SMS.

Each Teyraa movement is regulated in five positions and tested for thermal stability. Our master horologists perform a 3-step quality verification on heart-rate regularity, power reserve, and water resistance before certification.

To preserve the legacy of your timepiece, we recommend a professional service every 5 years. This includes cleaning, lubrication of the movement, and seal replacement to ensure lifelong accuracy.
